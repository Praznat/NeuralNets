INTRO: This is a game that pits you (the player) against a neural network AI opponent. The player takes actions by pressing the action buttons (or pressing the corresponding keys). The AI tries to guess the next action the player takes based on a memory of previous actions. The player outsmarts the AI by taking actions that the AI fails to guess. The AI will generally do badly at first but gradually learns the player's behavior. You can cheat (and verify the AI is not cheating) by looking at the output of the neural network.
